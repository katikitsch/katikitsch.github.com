WELCOME

Revolve is a vintage clothing store that has a great selection of men and women's clothing ranging from the early 1900's to the early 90's with most of our items being from the 70's and 80's. Whether you're looking for that super soft thin t-shirt that they just don't make anymore, that dress from the 80's that seemed like a mistake then (and probably was), broken in leathers, western snap shirts, disco shirts, hippie skirts, belts, belt buckles, TONS of colorful blouses, sunglasses, jewelry, POLYESTER! ...Well, you get the picture. This place is pretty fun and your one stop shop for that unique item you just can't find anywhere else. Come check us out!
