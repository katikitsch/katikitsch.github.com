Mountain Girl Clothing

Revolve Vintage is excited to announce that on top of our wide variety of vintage items, we are now carrying Mountain Girl Clothing. Click HERE to see some of her creations!

Here are a few words from the mountain girl herself explaining what "Mountain Girl Clothing" is all about:

"Hello! All clothing I create is either made of recycled or organic materials. I love using forgotten and unloved items of clothing and giving them new life. There are so many beautiful textures and fabrics and items of clothing that already exist in the world. I do not see the need to make demands on the already non mother earth friendly textile businesses that exist. My life revolves around these clothing creations and the joy I get from sewing each piece. All of my materials comes from either the salvation army, goodwill, community rummage sales or yard sales so all of the money that I spend towards my business goes towards my community and organizations that benefit the greater good."
