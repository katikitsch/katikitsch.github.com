General Membership

Join today!

When you become a member of the Lyman Allyn Art Museum, you join a special group of people who care about the arts in our community and support what they believe in.


Members enjoy benefits like Members Only* exhibition preview receptions, priority registration and discounts on programs, special events, and Museum store purchases. Members receive advance notice of Museum happenings and program offerings. And, of course, members get free admission to all special exhibitions as often as they like. Download a museum membership form. You may also join or renew in the Museum lobby during regular Museum hours or by calling Roberta Palmer, Monday–Friday, 9:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. at 860.443.2545 ext. 132.


Categories and Benefits

All members receive these benefits….

  • FREE Admission to the Museum and Deshon Allyn House
  • Invitations to Members Only* exhibitions, receptions and Museum events
  • Subscription to Columns, the Museum's newsletter
  • 10% discount in Museum store
  • Subscription to enewsletter
  • Discounts on lectures, classes, trips and other Museum programs
  • Member decal/sticker
  • Members Only* Double Discount Days
  • Free Admission to Ten (10) New England Museums
  • Tax deductible as allowed by law


Membership Levels

Student: $25, Individual: $40, Family/Dual: $60

  • All Member benefits listed above
  • FREE Admission for two adults at same address and children/grandchildren, 18 years and under
  • Discounts on Birthday Party rentals
  • Advance priority enrollment in family programs and workshops

Friend of the Museum: $100

  • All Member benefits listed above
  • Two (2) complimentary guest passes

Sustaining Member: $250

  • All Member benefits listed above
  • Two (2) additional complimentary guest passes (4 total)
  • Individual gift membership

Patron: $500

  • All Member benefits listed above
  • Two (2) additional complimentary guest passes (4 total)
  • Family gift membership
  • Special Lyman Allyn gift

Want to join at a higher level? Check out all the great benefits you will receive with a Harriet Allyn Society Membership.


Employer Matching Gifts may be available at your company. You can increase your giving and membership level! Check with your human resources department.
