Adult Group Tours



Regular museum admission: $8.00 adults/ $7.00 seniors & students


Group admission (available for groups of ten or more) with hour-long guided tour: $9.00 per person.


Hour-long guided tours of the permanent collection or special exhibitions may be reserved with at least two weeks advanced notice.


Please contact the Education Department for more information or to make a reservation: 860-443-2545 x110 or email clarke@lymanallyn.org.
