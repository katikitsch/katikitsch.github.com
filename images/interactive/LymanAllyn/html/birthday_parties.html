Birthday Parties

Party Arty!

Click here to download party invitation!

The museum is a very special place for a birthday party! The standard birthday party is a two-hour rental at the Museum. During the first hour of the festivities, a facilitator works with party guests, leading looking games in the galleries and a hands-on sculpture activity. The second hour of the party is reserved for celebrating in the Museum's beautiful Auditorium.


The cost of a birthday party for 12 guests is $120 for members/$140 for non-members. There is an additional charge of $10 per guest after the initial 12. This price includes the rental of the space, the facilitator, and the materials for the art activity. A 50% deposit is required within one week of booking a party. The balance is paid on the day of the party. The Museum accepts Visa, Mastercard, checks, and cash.


Families are allowed to begin decorating the birthday space one hour prior to the party. A refrigerator is available on-site for party use.

The cost of a birthday party does not include food, utensils, table cloths or gift bags.


To book your party please contact Mollie Clarke at (860) 443-2545 x 110 or email Clarke@lymanallyn.org. In order to secure your preferred date it is recommended that you call three weeks in advance.


The museum is also available for other parties and events! If you would like to host a shower, bar/bat mitzvah, holiday party, anniversary party, wedding, or reception at the Museum, please contact Lorraine Gruber at (860) 443-2545 x 112 or gruber@lymanallyn.org.
