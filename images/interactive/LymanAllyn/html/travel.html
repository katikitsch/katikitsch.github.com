Travel with the Lyman Allyn

Bus Trip to the Whitney Museum of American Art
in New York

Wednesday, April 6th (8:30am-6:30pm)
$70 members/$80 non-members
Click to download application


Join us for a splendid day in New York City as we explore the Whitney Museum of American Art, which features the blockbuster show: Modern Life- Edward Hopper and His Time. This temporary exhibit traces the development of realism in American art between 1900 and 1940, emphasizing the diverse ways that artists depicted the sweeping transformations in urban and rural life that occurred during this period. You may self-guide the exhibition and the permanent collection or join one of the numerous guided tours that the museum offers throughout the day (a list of these tour times will be provided on the bus). Lunch is on your own at the museum café or in one of the many restaurants in the city. Note: the bus will leave the Lyman Allyn parking lot at 8:30am and return at approximately 6:30pm.


Space is limited and advanced reservations are required. Please contact Mollie Clarke, Director of Education at 860-443-2545 x110 or return the attached registration form to reserve your space.
